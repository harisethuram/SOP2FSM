Perturbation Log
================
22 controlled perturbations across 13 SOPs in 3 domains.

Perturbation Types (3 categories):
  R = Within-SOP redundancy
      A new step that semantically duplicates an existing step in the same SOP,
      expressed with different wording.
  I = Cross-SOP inconsistency
      A check that means the same thing as a check in another SOP within the same
      organization, but has diverged — different wording, different thresholds,
      different structural granularity, or different ancillary requirements. Simulates
      the kind of drift that occurs when procedures are maintained independently.
  F = False positive
      A step worded very similarly to a step in another SOP or domain, but with a
      genuinely different semantic meaning. Tests whether the system can distinguish
      surface-level textual similarity from true semantic equivalence.
