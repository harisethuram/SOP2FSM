Perturbation Log
================
16 controlled perturbations across 13 SOPs in 3 domains.

Perturbation Types:
  W = Within-SOP redundancy (new step semantically duplicates an existing step in the same SOP)
  C = Cross-SOP redundancy (new step duplicates a check from another SOP in the same org)
  N = Near-duplicate canonical check (wording is close to a check in another SOP/org, testing embedding similarity)
  S = Structural variation (an existing combined step is split into finer-grained sub-steps)
